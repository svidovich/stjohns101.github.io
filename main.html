Welcome to St. John's United Church of Christ! We are a non-denominational Christian church located in the heart of downtown Massillon, on the corner of 1st and Tremont. Our services are Bible based and always inspirational, combining the old and the new.
     We have many outreach programs, including a free taxable goods giveaway, food pantry, and community meal, as well as a growing youth program. If you'd like to find out more about our church, navigate to the 'Contact Us' page and give us a call!

Sunday services begin at 10:30 AM, and Sunday School begins at 9:30 AM.
Looking for bible study? Come in each Sunday at 9:00 to hear the best of the scripture!
